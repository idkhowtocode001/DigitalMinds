About Us

The Digital Minds is an intensive programme for individuals interested in making significant contributions to research on AI consciousness, AI welfare, and the societal implications of digital minds.

Leadership

Our leadership team brings together experts from various fields to guide Digital Minds in its mission to advance research on AI consciousness and societal implications.

  • Tom Cook

    CEO

    Prior to joining Digital Minds, Tom was vice president of Corporate Materials for Compaq and was responsible for procuring and managing all of Compaq’s product inventory.

  • John Ternus

    Senior Vice President

    Prior to Digital Minds, John worked as a mechanical engineer at Virtual Research Systems. He holds a bachelor’s degree in Mechanical Engineering from the University of Pennsylvania.

  • Tor Myhren

    Marketing Communications

    Tor Myhren is Digital Minds’ vice president of Marketing Communications, reporting to CEO Tom Cook.
